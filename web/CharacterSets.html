Anything Missing?

We would like to additional fonts to the Ethiopic Library. If you have information about fonts from: Abugida, Ecological Linguistics, Ethio 2000, Laser Amharic, Jarinda World Trade, or know of other fonts not listed here, please contact us to help us support these products.

In the Works...

The next wave of work will be aimed at input for the "Dehai", "Ed", "Ethiop", "ISO/TC46/SC2", "Mainz", and "Qubee" systems. Since the discovering of "Extended Ed" Ed input support will mean addressing v0.34 issues of Extended Ethiopic and hence may take longer.
